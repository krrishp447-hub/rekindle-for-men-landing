## 1. BRAND CORE (THE FOUNDATION)

- **Category:** Candyceutical™ (clinical nutraceuticals delivered as dark chocolate).
- **Core Idea:** Healing should feel like something you look forward to.
- **Brand Promise:** Where science meets nature, wrapped in pleasure.
- **Target Audience:** Urban Indian women (28–55) managing PMS, PCOS, or Perimenopause. They value aesthetics, credibility, and want to reclaim control without medical dependency.
- **Tone of Voice:** Empathetic, warm, scientifically grounded, slightly rebellious. 
  *(Rules: Use "you", keep it conversational, use em dashes. Avoid sounding clinical, over-explaining, or using jargon.)*

## 2. PSYCHOLOGICAL POSITIONING (THE REFRAME)

- **The Core Insight:** People don’t fail supplements. Supplements fail consistency.
- **The Ultimate Reframe ("The Enemy"):** "It’s not you. It’s the format." Capsules are forgotten; powders taste bad.
- **The Solution:** A daily self-care ritual. A clinically-backed indulgence. (Selling the concept of "You'll actually stick to this" rather than just the ingredient list).

## 3. DESIGN SYSTEM (VISUAL REPRESENTATION)

### Color Palette
The brand avoids harsh contrasts. It feels warm, premium, and calm.
- **Primary (Luxury/Health):** Burgundy `#8B1A1A` 
- **Backgrounds (Base):** Cream `#F5F0E8` *(Rule: Never use pure white backgrounds)*
- **Text / CTA Default:** Charcoal `#1A1A1A`
- **Accents (Highlights/Trust):** Amber `#C97A3A`
*(Rule: Never use cold tones or sterile medical blues).*

### Typography
- **Headings:** Georgia (Premium, editorial, trustworthy)
- **Body:** Calibri (Modern, clean, legible)
*(Rule: Avoid ALL CAPS to maintain a calm, conversational feel).*

### Layout Rules
- **Structure:** Airy, high whitespace, breathing room between sections.
- **Container:** Centered (max 1280px width on desktop).
- **UI Elements:** Pill-shaped buttons (soft, human).

### Photography Guidelines
- **Lighting:** Warm light.
- **Subjects:** Indian women (30–55), conveying calm, composure, and control.
- **Backgrounds:** Neutral, soft environments.
*(Warnings: Absolutely no gym aesthetics, no cold clinical setups, no standard Western stock imagery).*

## 4. INTERACTION & DEVELOPMENT PRINCIPLES

- **The Feeling:** Calm, premium, intentional (not loud, fast, or salesy).
- **Animations:** Fade-in scroll effects, soft hover states, smooth transitions. No aggressive popping or shaking.
- **Frontend Performance:** Lightning fast (<2s load time), lazy images, minimal JS overhead. Tailwind CSS + semantic HTML.
- **Mobile First:** Thumb-friendly CTA sizing, large readable text, clean vertical spacing.
